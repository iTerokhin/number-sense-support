FAQ

How do I play?

You receive numbers from 1 to 1000. Each number must be placed into one of the available tiles. The goal is to keep the tiles ordered from bottom to top. Once placed, numbers cannot be moved.

How do levels work?

The game starts with a single tile. Each new level adds more tiles, up to 20. As the number of tiles increases, relying on intuition becomes essential.
